Anonymous. Secure. Two-way communications.

Whispli offers a range of business solutions based on a secure, anonymous, two-way communications platform. Whispli’s high grade encryption and security allows people to give reports and information anonymously. And the two-way communication means you can get all the information you need to manage the case securely, easily and cost-effectively.

WHISPLI IS:Flexible, configurable, customisable.

Whispli’s platform is highly configurable. We can configure it to fit your unique need for secure two way communication. And it’s self-serve, so you can customise the forms and other features yourself. You can even whitelabel it to onsell to your customers. What’s more, you can choose to manage it inhouse, or outsource the case management to a third party.

WHISPLI IS:Cloudbased SaaS.

Whispli uses a cloudbased Software as a Service (Saas) distribution model, which means you can track and manage all files and information in one secure place. No more emails, spreadsheets or missing documents. And because it’s subscription based, you only pay for active users. No complex or expensive software installations. In fact, you can implement it yourself within minutes.
